About Me

I am 21 years old international and trilingual engineering student. Originally from the Netherlands, I lived in Spain for several years before embarking on a Systems & Control Engineering degree at the University of Sheffield in the UK. Having completed my year abroad at the National University of Singapore, I am currently on my year in industry as a Systems Engineer Intern at GE Aviation. These experiences have taught me to be an independent, reliable and energetic member of the community, allowing me to develop both technical knowledge and commercial awareness through a variety of extra-curricular activities and short work placements, at institutions including Aon Risk Solutions and The Singapore University of Technology and Design.

Work Experience

Systems Engineer Intern at General Electric Aviation | July 2015 – Present | Cheltenham, United Kingdom

I'm working as a Systems Engineer gaining invaluable skills and experiences, through opportunities covering all aspects from the design of systems to testing. My main focus is on analysing requirements, creating a system design or defining a test specification. Giving me the responsibility to deliver on inspiring projects.

Research Intern at The Singapore University of Technology and Design | January 2015 – March 2015 | Singapore, Singapore

The Singapore University of Technology and Design is established in collaboration with MIT. I am currently developing the first and second phase of multi-sensor embedded systems in a multi-national research team. 80,000 of these systems will be deployed in Singapore which will be the largest single deployment in the world of its kind. This will be used by the National Research Foundation of the Singaporean Government for big data analysis into cultural integration.

Spring Intern at Aon Risk Solutions | April 2014 – April 2014 | Moscow, Russian Federation

2 weeks insight into the Russian insurance market with both Aon Plc and Ace group through work shadowing. Shadowed senior meetings regarding freight claims of the 2014 Sochi Winter Olympic Games. Analysed maritime hull claims valued over £2M. Highlighted potential clients for D&O in combination with IPO policies in the Russian Federation.
